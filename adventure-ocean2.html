Descend the cliff


After a few minutes of searching, you find a steep, rocky path down to the water. You make your way down carefully, knocking a few small stones down in front of you. At the bottom, you see that the shiny thing in the sand is a large golden scale; too big to be from a fish. You notice that the tide is coming in, and know that when it gets high enough, the ocean will reclaim this prize.
