Explore the beach


You wander around the little beach and see a few clusters of rocks with tide pools that have snails, tiny crabs, and seaweed. Every so often a big enough wave will breach the rocks and rush into one of the pools, creating little whirls of bubbles in the still water. At the ocean's edge you stare out at the horizon and see the silhouette of a sailboat, but no other ships.
